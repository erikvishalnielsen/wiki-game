TETRIS STRATEGY

The Basic Rules

  • You bring down blocks from the top of the screen
  • You can move the blocks around, either left/right or you can rotate them
  • The blocks fall at a certain rate, but you can make them fall faster
  • When the blocks fill a horizontal line, they dissapear and you gain points
  • If your blocks reach the top of the screen you loose

The Scoring Guidelines
